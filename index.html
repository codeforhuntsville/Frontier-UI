About Frontier

Frontier is an app developed by Code for Huntsville that lets you give it your location and then returns a list of accessible resources nearby. For example, it might tell you the nearest open restaurants, the nearest open gas stations, the nearest public wifi hotspots, the nearest public restrooms, or the nearest open towing/mechanic shops.
